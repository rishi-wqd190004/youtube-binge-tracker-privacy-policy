Privacy Policy for YouTube Binge Tracker Extension

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the YouTube Binge Tracker extension.

Data We Collect

  • Video Count: We track the number of YouTube videos you watch while using the extension.
  • Elapsed Time: We track how much time you spend watching videos.
  • Last Video ID: We track the ID of the last video you watched to prevent counting the same video more than once.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to display a counter on the page, showing the number of videos you’ve watched and the time you’ve spent watching them. Your data is stored locally within the browser and is not shared with any external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API or localStorage. This data is only accessible within your browser and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data, including using browser storage methods that are secure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Control

You can manage your data by clearing the extension's local storage or uninstalling the extension.
